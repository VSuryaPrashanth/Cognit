About us

The Association of Computer Engineers, AI Engineers and Data Scientists (ACE & AIE) of MNMJEC are delighted to present to you the 9th season of our Annual National-Level Technical Symposium: COGNIT.
COGNIT is a National Level Technical Symposium; aimed to challenge all 8 of your COGNITive skills and develop them along the way. It encompasses of a network of tricky and rigorous Technical events as well as refreshing and exuberant Non-Technical events that are designed to leave you, the participant thoroughly stimulated and believing in the spirit of our tagline “COMPETE TO INSPIRE”.
